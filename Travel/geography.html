Some of Our Favorite Places Around the World

Are you interested in exploring Asia? Learning about the travel opportunities in different parts of Africa? Learn about some of our favorite places we've visited!

New Zealand

We must love New Zealand, because we've both been there three times (twice together, once independently)! There's a reason this country was chosen as the setting for Middle Earth; everywhere you look there's stunning scenery. Some of our favorite experiences in New Zealand include:

The Tongariro Crossing

The Milford Track

The Kepler Track

Sky Diving the Southern Alps

Via Ferrata

White Water Rafting

Hiking Fox Glacier

Of course, there's so much else to do there as well, from cherry picking to white water rafting to a fabulous feast at Hobbiton.


Patagonia (Chile and Argentina)

We've been here once and can't wait to go back. (In fact, the trip is already planned--now to find the time!) Between completing the multi-day W Trek in Chile's Torres Del Paine and conquering multiple rigorous hikes in Argentina's El Chalten/Glaciares National Park, there's so much beautiful scenery to soak in.
