Expedition Cruises

Expedition cruises are one of the best ways to see wildlife and experience locations that are out of reach for most people. We've gone on two so far and are eager to do more. Our first was with Quark Expeditions, aboard their new Ultramarine, sailing the Arctic near Svalbard. Our second was with Viking on their beautiful Octantis.
