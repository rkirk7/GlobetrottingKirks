About Mei-Mei and Regan Kirk

We are a mother-daughter duo who love traveling together and are eager to share the benefit of our experience. We've hiked New Zealand, Patagonia, Nepal, and many national parks in the U.S.; we've gone on numerous safaris and have had amazing animal experiences in the Galapagos, the Arctic, and Antarctica; we've enjoyed numerous cruises around the world, from luxury to more family-friendly cruise lines; and we've enjoyed exploring beautiful, interesting locations around the world.

About Mei-Mei

I’m a retired media exec who has been passionately exploring the world for the past decade and helping others find their way. I’ve been to 70-plus countries and all seven continents, having fallen in love with Antarctica in 2024. My favorite place? Africa, where I’ll return for my 7th time in December.

At 65, my adventurous spirit is stronger than ever. But I like my luxuries, too. My personal expertise, discerning scrutiny, and tireless research produce show-stopping experiences that are unforgettable. I'd love to help make your bucket list dreams come true!
